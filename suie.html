E-commerce from the ground up

In this group project, we developed SUIE – a conceptual e-commerce brand specializing in scarfs. Our mission was to build a business-ready foundation, covering everything from Shopify development and target audience analysis to a zero budget marketing strategy. A major focus was placed on financial scalability, where we calculated margins (GM1, GM2, GM3) to ensure longterm profitability and production readiness.
