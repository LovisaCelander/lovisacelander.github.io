Rethinking accessibility in cultural spaces.

Worked in a team to respond to a brief from Fotografiska focused on making the world’s most open museum even more accessible. Through research and user insight, we identified gaps in accessibility for visually impaired visitors and developed a concept for an AI-based companion that would guide users through exhibitions in a narrative, adaptive voice experience.

Beyond the experience concept, the project also included communication strategy and a PR driven activation idea to strengthen awareness around accessibility in cultural environments, ensuring the museum’s message reaches a broader, more inclusive audience.
